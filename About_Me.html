Synopsis

My name is Frank Lewis Fox Junior. I was born in 1980 in Wiesbaden, Hessa, Germany. My father was stationed there as a Mastersergeant in the U.S. Air Force. I've lived in two countries and 4 U.S. states, and attended 7 different schools, all before I was 11. As a kid I loved Legos, fishing, riding my bike, Rollerblading, and taking road trips with my parents. When I was 11, we settled in Arizona and I've been here ever since. After settling here I was finally able to set down roots, collect long term friends, eventually meet my wife, and call Phoenix home.
